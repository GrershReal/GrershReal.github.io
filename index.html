Pressure Cooker Studio

Pressure Cooker Studio is a Game Development Studio originating on Roblox founded by Grersh with UI and Logo Design assistance from Synthlax. Established around 2020, Effortful Studio, now known as Pressure Cooker Studio, began as a small side project for small scale games made by Grersh.
Now, 5 years later, Pressure Cooker Studio's mission is to create high quality, fun and engaging games. All whilst maintaining and creating open-source content for fellow developers to utilize.
